# gFractor
**A powerful and flexible spectrum analyzer for modern audio production.**

## Description

A spectrum analyzer is one of the most essential tools in audio production—from sound design to mixing and mastering. gFractor gives you precise visual feedback and deep insight into your audio at every stage of the workflow.

With advanced analysis modes, flexible filtering, and detailed stereo metering, gFractor helps you understand your sound instantly. A clean, elegant interface keeps everything clear and accessible, so you can focus on what matters most: making better decisions, faster.

Whether you're shaping transients, balancing stereo content, or comparing signals, gFractor delivers powerful analysis with effortless usability.

## Key Features

### Advanced Analysis Modes
Switch between three powerful analysis modes to explore your signal from different perspectives:

- **Mid / Side**
- **Left / Right**
- **Transient / Tone**

### Flexible Band-Pass Filtering
Focus on exactly what you want to analyze:

- Adjustable **frequency** and **Q**
- Quick **frequency range selection**

### Component Monitoring
Listen to and visualize individual signal components:

- Monitor **Mid** and **Side** separately
- Isolate **Transient** and **Tonal** content

### Signal Comparison
Analyze relationships between signals with ease:

- Visual comparison of **Main** and **Sidechain**
- Instantly swap **Main ↔ Sidechain** with a key press

### Stereo Analysis
Comprehensive stereo visualization tools:

- **Goniometer**
- **Correlation meter**
- **Stereo width per octave**

### Spectrum Control
Advanced spectrum display tools for precise analysis:

- **Freeze spectrum**
- **Peak hold**
- Quick access to **Slope**, **Decay**, **Overlap**, and **FFT settings**

### Transient Detection
Fine-tune transient analysis with **adjustable capture time** for more accurate visualization.

### Customization
Adapt the interface to your workflow:

- Multiple **themes**
- Fully **adjustable colors**
